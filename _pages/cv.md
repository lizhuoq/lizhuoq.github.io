Projects
======
* **Deep Learning Models for [Climsim](https://github.com/leap-stc/ClimSim)**, machine learning models that accurately emulate subgrid-scale atmospheric physics in an operational climate model, ([GitHub](https://github.com/lizhuoq/kaggle_climsim_35th))
* **LSTS (land surface time sereies)**, LSTS is an open-source tool written in Python, utilizing advanced deep learning algorithms such as RNN-based LSTM and EALSTM, MLP-based DLinear, CNN-based TimesNet, and Transformer-based iTransformer and PatchTST models. It provides univariate short- and longterm forecasting and imputation tasks for land surface variables such as soil temperature, soil moisture, soil suction, snow depth, snow water equivalent, air temperature, and surface temperature. The advantages of our software include speed, lightweight, accuracy, and universality. ([Github](https://github.com/lizhuoq/land_surface_time_series), [Huggingface](https://huggingface.co/lizhuoqun/land_surface_time_series), [Pypi](https://pypi.org/project/lsts/))
* **WeatherLearn**, Implementation of the PyTorch version of the Weather Deep Learning Model Zoo. ([Github](https://github.com/lizhuoq/WeatherLearn))
